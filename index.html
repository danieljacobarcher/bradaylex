the cause

BREAST RECONSTRUCTION AWARENESS
CAN CHANGE REAL LIVES.

Breast Reconstruction Awareness Day, or BRA Day, is an initiative designed to promote education, awareness and access regarding post-mastectomy breast reconstruction.

Women concerned about breast cancer are often unaware of their breast reconstruction options.

When making one of the most personal choices of their lives, breast cancer patients should know they have options. This should be discussed with their plastic surgeon prior to any type of surgery. In the USA, 7 out of 10 women are not aware of their breast reconstruction options following mastectomy. In Kentucky, the number is greater. Less than 1/5 of American women who undergo mastectomy go on to have reconstruction. 89% of women would want to see what breast reconstruction results look like before making a treatment decision.

The Women's Health and Cancer Rights Act of 1998 requires health insurers to cover the cost of breast reconstruction following mastectomy in all 50 states.

  • 10/
    2011

    BRA DAY Started

    Breast Reconstruction Awareness Day was first launched in Canada in 2011, founded to promote education, awareness, and access regarding post-mastectomy breast reconstruction.

  • 10/
    2012

    Emerging across the US

    BRA day was launched in the USA last October at the American Association for Plastic Surgeons national meeting in New Orleans. Jewel introduced and sang "Flower", a song she specifically wrote to help raise awareness for breast reconstruction. Thousands of dollars were raised to help women with breast cancer be reconstructed.

  • 03/
    2013

    Local Lexington Women

    Early last year, The Center for Plastic Surgery brought thirteen breast cancer fighters together to introduce Lexington to BRA Day. Since then, these talented and inspirational women are working hard to teach the benefits of post-mastectomy breast reconstruction and help others realize their potential. Their stories will be published in a book entitled "Looking Good to Feel Good" with beautiful photography by Scott Walz. It is a book which will be dedicated to all women with breast cancer across Kentucky.

  • 10/
    2013

    BRA DAY LEX Introduction

    Last October, we launched our inaugural event that supported the cause, featuring breast cancer honorees, live entertainment, a hosted bar, and a silent auction. It was a magnificent event.

  • 10/16
    2014

    BRA DAY LEX Returns

    This October, we're returning to bring support to breast cancer fighters, more exciting entertainment, education, and even more surprises to come!
